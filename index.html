Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your project.

Our projects span a wide range depending on scope and finishes:

  • Under ₹5,00,000 — Basic fit-out & soft furnishing
  • ₹5,00,000 – ₹10,00,000 — Standard modular design
  • ₹10,00,000 – ₹25,00,000 — Full residential design
  • ₹25,00,000 – ₹50,00,000 — Premium design with custom furniture
  • ₹50,00,000 – ₹1,00,00,000+ — Luxury turnkey projects

We work with every budget and always recommend a consultation first so we can tailor the proposal to your needs.

We are fluent in all major interior design styles including:

  • Modern & Contemporary — clean lines, neutral palettes
  • Minimalist — less is more, decluttered spaces
  • Scandinavian — warm woods, functional elegance
  • Mid-Century Modern — retro with organic forms
  • Industrial — raw materials, exposed elements
  • Traditional & Classic — rich details, timeless appeal
  • Eclectic & Transitional — a curated mix of styles

During consultation, we help you identify your preferred style through mood boards and visual references.

We handle all residential configurations:

  • 1 BHK, 2 BHK, 3 BHK, 4 BHK apartments
  • Villas & independent houses
  • Duplexes & penthouses
  • Commercial & office spaces
  • Hospitality & F&B outlets

Whether it is a compact studio or a sprawling villa, our team designs for every space optimally.

Timelines vary by project size and complexity, but general estimates are:

  • 1 BHK / 2 BHK — 6 to 10 weeks
  • 3 BHK / 4 BHK — 10 to 16 weeks
  • Villa / Large Residence — 16 to 28 weeks
  • Commercial Turnkey — Depends on area and scope

We share a detailed project schedule after the initial consultation and concept approval.

We design every area of your home or commercial space, including:

  • Living Room — sofa, TV unit, false ceiling, lighting, partitions
  • Dining Room — table, chairs, bar unit, wallpaper & paint
  • Kitchen — modular layouts, appliances, countertops, storage
  • Master & other Bedrooms — bed, wardrobes, study, dressing
  • Bathrooms — vanity, fixtures, tiles, accessories
  • Home Office, Pooja Room, Kids' Room, Balcony & more

We work at any stage of your property journey:

  • Bare Shell — we design from scratch alongside construction
  • Under Construction — we plan ahead so execution is seamless on possession
  • Ready to Move In — we begin immediately with full fit-out
  • Existing Home / Renovation — we assess the space and redesign with minimal disruption

We source from premium vendors across all material categories:

  • Flooring — Marble, Tiles, Engineered Wood, Vinyl, Carpet
  • Walls — Wallpaper, Textured Paint, Paneling, Stone cladding
  • Furniture — Custom-made, modular, and curated from top brands
  • Lighting — Recessed, pendant, cove, statement & smart lighting

All materials are selected to match your chosen design style and budget.

Getting started is simple — just 3 steps:

  • 1. Fill the enquiry form — share your name, location, flat config, possession status & scope of work
  • 2. Free consultation — our designer meets you (in person or virtually) to understand your vision
  • 3. Proposal & kickoff — we present concept designs, get your approval, and begin execution

Use the contact form above or WhatsApp us directly to get started today.
