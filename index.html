How We Work

Customer On-Boarding Process

A transparent 12-step journey from first meeting to final handover â€” so you always know what comes next.

01

Understanding Customer Requirement

  • First meeting with the client
  • Discuss needs and expectations
  • Understand budget and timeline
  • Confirm project timeline
  • Note lifestyle, preferences, and space usage
02

Concept & Theme Discussion

  • Show different interior design themes
  • Understand client's style and preferences
  • Discuss colors, materials, and finishes
  • Suggest the best-suited design concept
  • Align design with budget and lifestyle
03

Site Visit & Measurement

  • Designer visits the site
  • Take accurate measurements
  • Check site condition
  • Capture photo and video for reference
  • Collect all technical details for design planning
04

Concept Drafting & Designing

  • Prepare the actual design concept
  • Create layout and design drawings
  • Finalize theme, colors, and materials
  • Share design ideas with the client
  • Make changes based on client feedback
05

BOQ / BOM â€” Rough Estimation

  • Prepare BOQ / BOM (list of materials)
  • Estimate approximate project cost
  • Explain cost breakup to the client
  • Adjust scope as per budget if required
  • Get approval to move ahead
06

Final Quote

  • Prepare final BOQ and quotation
  • Confirm final design, materials, and pricing
  • Share complete cost details
  • Get client's final approval
  • Proceed to execution stage
07

2D Drawing Making

  • Prepare detailed 2D drawings
  • Layout planning and elevations
  • Furniture and cabinet details
  • Finalize material placements
  • Use drawings for execution clarity
08

3D Drawing Making

  • Create 3D views based on approved 2D drawings
  • Show actual colors, materials, and finishes
  • Help client visualize the final look
  • Make revisions if required
  • Get final design approval
09

Material Procurement @ Market

  • Visit market with the client
  • Select materials, finishes, and fittings
  • Finalize brands and specifications
  • Procure approved materials
  • Ensure quality and budget control
10

Work Execution

  • Start actual on-site work
  • Follow approved drawings and designs
  • Work under expert supervision
  • Regular quality checks
  • Timely progress updates to client
11

Photoshoot

  • Professional photoshoot after completion
  • Capture final interiors and details
  • Use images for records and social media
  • Share selected photos with the client
12

Handover ðŸŽ‰

  • Final site inspection with the client
  • Handover of completed project
  • Keys handed over to the client
  • Small handover celebration
  • Project successfully closed
